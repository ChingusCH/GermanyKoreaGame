Introducing Yourself in German

Introducing yourself in German is an important basic skill for everyday communication, travel, and meeting new people. It usually includes your name, age, origin, and sometimes hobbies or occupation.

Basic Introductions

Ich heiße... – My name is...

Ich bin... – I am...

Mein Name ist... – My name is...

Talking About Yourself

Ich komme aus... – I come from...

Ich bin ... Jahre alt. – I am ... years old.

Ich wohne in... – I live in...

Meeting Someone New

Freut mich! – Nice to meet you!

Wie heißt du? / Wie heißen Sie? – What is your name?

Cultural Significance

In Germany, polite introductions are important in both formal and informal situations. Using correct greetings and clear self-introductions helps create a respectful first impression.
