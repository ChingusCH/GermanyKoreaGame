Korean Sentence Structure Quiz

Click an option to see if you are correct!

1. What is the basic word order in Korean sentences?

Correct Answer: c) S–O–V

2. In the sentence 저는 밥을 먹어요, what is the object?

Correct Answer: b) 밥을 (rice)

3. Which particle marks the subject of a sentence?

Correct Answer: b) 이 / 가

4. Where do adjectives usually appear in Korean?

Correct Answer: b) Before the noun

5. In Korean, when do time and place usually appear?

Correct Answer: a) Before the verb

6. Why are verb endings important in Korean?

Correct Answer: a) They show politeness and social hierarchy
