German Literature Quiz

1. What is the Nibelungenlied?

Correct Answer: b) A medieval epic poem

2. Which period focused on reason and education?

Correct Answer: b) Enlightenment

3. Which two writers are central to Weimar Classicism?

Correct Answer: b) Goethe and Schiller

4. Which literary movement emphasized emotion and nature?

Correct Answer: c) Romanticism

5. Which author is known for works reflecting war and exile?

Correct Answer: b) Thomas Mann

6. What role does literature play in German culture today?

Correct Answer: c) It shapes education and cultural identity
