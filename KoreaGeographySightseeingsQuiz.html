Sightseeing Quiz - South Korea

Test your knowledge about famous sightseeing spots in South Korea. Click an option to check your answer!

1. Gyeongbokgung Palace is associated with which Korean dynasty?

Correct Answer: b) Joseon

2. What is a hanok?

Correct Answer: a) A traditional Korean house

3. N Seoul Tower is located on which mountain?

Correct Answer: c) Namsan

4. Which destination is known for volcanic landscapes and lava tubes?

Correct Answer: b) Jeju Island

5. Gyeongju is often called “the museum without walls” because it contains

Correct Answer: c) numerous outdoor historical sites

6. Gamcheon Culture Village is famous for

Correct Answer: b) colorful hillside homes and murals
