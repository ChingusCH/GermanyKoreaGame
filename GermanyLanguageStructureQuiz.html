German Sentence Structure Quiz

Click an option to check your answer. Correct answers will be highlighted!

1. What is the typical word order in a simple German main clause?

Correct Answer: a) Subject-Verb-Object

2. In a yes/no question, where does the verb go?

Correct Answer: a) First

3. In sentences with time, manner, and place, what is the correct order?

Correct Answer: a) Time-Manner-Place

4. In a sentence with a separable verb, where does the prefix usually go?

Correct Answer: a) At the end of the sentence

5. In subordinate clauses introduced by weil, where is the verb?

Correct Answer: a) At the end

6. Why is mastering German sentence structure important?

Correct Answer: a) It helps communicate clearly and understand written and spoken German
