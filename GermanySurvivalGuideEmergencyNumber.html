Emergency Numbers in Germany

Knowing emergency numbers in Germany is essential for safety. In case of accidents, medical emergencies, or crimes, contacting the right service quickly can save lives. Germany uses specific short numbers for different types of emergencies.

Important Emergency Numbers

Police – 110
Call 110 for police assistance, reporting crimes, or requesting immediate help in dangerous situations.

Fire Department and Ambulance – 112
Call 112 for fires, accidents, or medical emergencies requiring urgent attention.

Other Services

Poison Control
030 19240 (Berlin; local numbers may vary by region)

Medical on-call service
116 117 (non-life-threatening medical help outside normal hours)

Cultural Significance

Germany places strong emphasis on safety and preparedness. These emergency numbers are widely known and accessible nationwide.
