German Dialects Quiz

1. What is Hochdeutsch mainly used for?

Correct Answer: c) Education, media, and official communication

2. Which dialect is mainly spoken in northern Germany?

Correct Answer: c) Low German (Plattdeutsch)

3. Which dialect is associated with Bavaria?

Correct Answer: c) Bavarian (Bairisch)

4. Where is Swabian mainly spoken?

Correct Answer: c) Southwestern Germany

5. Which dialect is linked to the Rhine region and carnival culture?

Correct Answer: d) Rhineland dialects (Kölsch)

6. Why are German dialects culturally important?

Correct Answer: b) They strengthen regional identity
