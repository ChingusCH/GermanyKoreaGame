Korean Street Food Quiz

Click an option to see if you are correct!

1. What is “Tteokbokki”?

Correct Answer: b) Spicy rice cakes

2. Which street food is a sweet pancake filled with sugar, nuts, and cinnamon?

Correct Answer: a) Hotteok

3. What is “Kimbap” often compared to?

Correct Answer: b) Sushi

4. What are “Odeng”?

Correct Answer: a) Fish cakes on skewers

5. Which street food is made with pig’s blood and glass noodles?

Correct Answer: a) Sundae

6. What is “Bungeoppang”?

Correct Answer: b) Fish-shaped pastry filled with sweet red bean or custard
