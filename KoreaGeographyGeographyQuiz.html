Short Geography Quiz - South Korea

Select the correct answer for each question. Click on an option to see if you are correct!

1. Which body of water lies to the east of South Korea?

Correct Answer: b) Sea of Japan (East Sea)

2. What is the name of the mountain range running along the eastern side of the peninsula?

Correct Answer: a) Taebaek Mountains

3. South Korea’s highest peak is located on which island?

Correct Answer: c) Jeju Island

4. Which river flows through Seoul?

Correct Answer: b) Han River

5. Most of South Korea’s rainfall occurs during which season?

Correct Answer: c) Summer
