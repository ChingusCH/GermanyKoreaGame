German Classical Music Quiz

1. Which composer is known for the Brandenburg Concertos?

Correct Answer: b) Bach

2. Which composer wrote the Ninth Symphony?

Correct Answer: c) Beethoven

3. Which musical period is Johann Sebastian Bach associated with?

Correct Answer: c) Baroque

4. Who is famous for epic operas like The Ring of the Nibelung?

Correct Answer: c) Wagner

5. Which composer is linked to the Romantic period?

Correct Answer: b) Brahms

6. What role does Germany play in classical music today?

Correct Answer: b) A global center for performance and education
