World Wars Quiz

1. Which countries did Germany fight with during World War I?

Correct Answer: b) The Central Powers

2. What was the Treaty of Versailles?

Correct Answer: b) A peace treaty after World War I

3. What government was established in Germany after World War I?

Correct Answer: c) Weimar Republic

4. What event marked the beginning of World War II in Europe?

Correct Answer: c) Germany’s invasion of Poland

5. Who led Germany during World War II?

Correct Answer: c) Adolf Hitler

6. What was a major consequence of World War II for Germany?

Correct Answer: b) Division into East and West Germany
