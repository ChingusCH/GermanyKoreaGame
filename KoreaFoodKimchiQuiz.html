Kimchi Varieties Quiz

Click an option to see if you are correct!

1. What is the most common type of kimchi made from napa cabbage?

Correct Answer: b) Baechu Kimchi

2. Which kimchi is made from cubed radish?

Correct Answer: c) Kkakdugi

3. Which kimchi is known as a watery, mild kimchi?

Correct Answer: b) Dongchimi

4. Which kimchi is made from cucumbers and is popular in summer?

Correct Answer: a) Oi Sobagi

5. What is “Chonggak Kimchi” made from?

Correct Answer: b) Small whole radishes with leafy tops

6. What is the traditional season for making large batches of kimchi called?

Correct Answer: c) Kimjang
