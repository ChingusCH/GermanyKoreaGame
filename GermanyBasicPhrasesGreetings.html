German Greetings

Greetings are an essential part of daily life in Germany. They are used in both formal and informal situations, and choosing the correct greeting shows respect and social awareness.

Common German Greetings

Hallo – General informal greeting (“hello”)

Guten Morgen – Good morning (until around noon)

Guten Tag – Good day (polite daytime greeting)

Guten Abend – Good evening (after ~6 PM)

Tschüss – Informal goodbye

Auf Wiedersehen – Formal goodbye (“until we see each other again”)

Wie geht’s? / Wie geht es Ihnen? – “How are you?” (informal / formal)

Cultural Significance

Using proper greetings in Germany reflects politeness and respect. Informal greetings are used with friends and family, while formal greetings are expected in professional and unfamiliar situations.
