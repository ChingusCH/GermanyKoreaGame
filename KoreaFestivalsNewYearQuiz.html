Seollal Quiz

Click an option to see if you are correct!

1. When is Seollal celebrated?

Correct Answer: b) First day of the lunar calendar

2. What is Charye?

Correct Answer: b) Ancestral rites

3. What is Sebae?

Correct Answer: b) The New Year’s bow to elders

4. Which food symbolizes getting a year older during Seollal?

Correct Answer: b) Tteokguk (rice cake soup)

5. What traditional games are played during Seollal?

Correct Answer: b) Yutnori, kite flying, spinning tops

6. What do elders usually give to children after Sebae?

Correct Answer: c) Money (세뱃돈, sebaetdon)
